About Tic-Tac Tow

Tic-Tac Tow has been providing professional towing and roadside assistance services throughout Orange County and Southern California since 2015. Our mission is to deliver fast, reliable, and affordable help to drivers in need.

With a fleet of modern, well-equipped trucks and a team of certified professionals, we handle everything from standard vehicle towing to specialized equipment transport. We pride ourselves on quick response times, transparent pricing, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

As your trusted Orange County towing company, we're available 24/7 for all your roadside emergency needs.
